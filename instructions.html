Educational Package

Our educational package, which includes a comprehensive instructions manual, can be used as a supplementary education tool for Engineering students in Sub-Saharan Africa.

We have included a parametric CAD model that allows users to customize their robot designs according to the motor dimensions in the e-waste they have found, and open-source code for controlling the robot.
