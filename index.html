Who are we?

Welcome to Not Rubbish Robotics - sustainability, education, innovation. We aim to inspire students in Sub-Saharan Africa to upcycle e-waste and build a robotic arm.

Through our robotic kit, we will teach a student how to salvage motors from old printers, and guide them through the process of building a robotic arm.

The students will then learn to control their robotic arm using our coding exercises.
